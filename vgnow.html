Video game Industry

New Advancements

The first video game was made in 1958 by a physicist, and was a simple tennis game. Now as technological advancements have evolved and improved, it has paved the way for more complex games to be released, moving from the 2D style of old video games and into 3D graphics. As time goes on improvements continue to be made to gaming, such as the devices used changing, from old slow computers to a headset that allows people to play a video game as if they were inside it.
The gaming industry as a business has also grown at an exponential rate, with the industry growing to around 138 billion dollars revenue since the first game was released. Some doctors are now even proscribing video games as a way to help hand eye coordination stress relief, problem solving skills and also helping to alleviate other various ailments like PTSD.
