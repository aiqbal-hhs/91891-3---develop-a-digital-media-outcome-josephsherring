Video game Industry

New Advancements

The Video game industry has ballooned to an incredible size over the past 10 years, with popularity increasing at an amazing rate, and video games overtaking the movie industry and the music industry respectively. This really shows how big of a business is around the world and how much of a big deal it is with entertainment for us to consume. The industry is valued at around 180 billion US dollars, compared to the movie industry which is a bit over 100 billion dollars which helps to put its popularity into perspective. The estimated amount of people who play video games is around 2.7 billion people which is around about 35% of the entire world population.
